  4. Harry Potter Series

    Harry Potter Series is a life of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The main story arc concerns Harry's struggle against Lord Voldemort, a dark wizard who intends to become immortal, overthrow the wizard governing body known as the Ministry of Magic, and subjugate all wizards and Muggles.

  5. Queen – Bollywood

    Queen is a journey of self transformation every woman, dumped at the altar or not, must take.

  6. Gravity

    Gravity is a story of astronauts who are stranded in space after the mid-orbit destruction of their space shuttle, and their subsequent attempt to return to Earth.

  7. Inception

    Dom Cobb is a thief with the rare ability to enter people's dreams and steal their secrets from their subconscious. His skill has made him a hot commodity in the world of corporate espionage but has also cost him everything he loves. Cobb gets a chance at redemption when he is offered a seemingly impossible task: Plant an idea in someone's mind. If he succeeds, it will be the perfect crime, but a dangerous enemy anticipates Cobb's every move.

  8. Taare Zameen Par – Bollywood

    The film explores the life and imagination of an 8-year-old dyslexic child and how his teacher helps him to overcome his disability.

  9. Interstellar

    A team of explorers travel through a wormhole in space in an attempt to ensure humanity's survival.

  10. WALL-E

    In the distant future, a small waste-collecting robot inadvertently embarks on a space journey that will ultimately decide the fate of mankind.
